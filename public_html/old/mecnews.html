Model Engineering College, Kochi was rated the Best College last year by AICTE based on their survey of the colleges in Kerala. The decision was made on the basis of the facilities in the college and strides made in the direction of producing quality engineers.

Placements for the Batch of 2002 have begun.CTS recruited 28 students, TATA Infotech has recruited one student and Paxonet Communications have recruited another two. Two students were recruited by SIEMENS via an external agency too.

In spite of the Global slowdown, there are more companies planning campus recruitment at MEC campus

The Indian Army has conducted a pre SSB screening session at the College. The Indian Air Force is soon to follow suit, application forms were collectd from the enthusiatic students.

IHRD decides to increase the number of seats in the Electronics and Computer streams from 60 to 120 in keeping with the risng demand for quality manpower for the various ventures scheduled to come up in the future in accordance with the latest IT policy of the government.

MECians won the overall championship in the CUSAT Sports and Games.

 

MECians came Third in the annual CUSAT University Arts Festival.

 

Model Engineering College holds a Inter-collegiate Arts Festival VARNAM 2000. The whole show was a success. This was MECians’ latest show after the very popular Rock Festival SATURNALIA which was discontinued later on.

A Technical festival was organised in the college under the aegis of the College Chapter of IEEE.

The Extraordinary meeting of the XMEC held on May 1st 2001 has decided that the eighth semester students of MEC would here on be active members of the Alumni Association.

Model Enginnering College was the Runners up in the very popular Inter-collegiate Art Festival TALENTIME 2000.
