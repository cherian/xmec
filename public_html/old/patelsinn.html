XMEC Bangalore Chapter Get Together at Patel's Inn

Date : Sunday, 18th May, 2003

Time : 10am to 5pm

The get together will include a buffet lunch and tea apart from a welcome drink in the morning. XMEcians can use the various facilities like shuttle courts, swimming pool, tennis court, basketball court, snooker table. Patels Inn will be offering a discounted rate of Rs 80 for the Go-Karting facility. There will be other entertaining games with prizes for the lucky winners .Please do not forget to bring your swim wear and cap if you plan to use the swiming pool. Bring in your cricket bats, volley ball and racquets

There are BMTC buses ( Route No 107C ) from Shivaji Nagar that stop very close to the resort(near Telephone Exchange). Apart from that there are frequent buses to RT Nagar.One can get down at the RT Nagar BDA stop and take an auto rickshaw to the resort.
