  IEEE Chapter of MEC to organise EXCEL 2K3...
The IEEE Chapter of MEC plans to organize the next edition of the Annual Technical Meet EXCEL 2K3 towards the first quarter of 2003. There are plans to ramp up the event into a South India Technical Event. While the ground work for the same have begun the right earnest, the event is tentatively planned for the 7th and 8th of March 2002. The Students Chapter of the IEEE requests the help and cooperation of all XMECians to make this a grand success. One can contact Varghese Eapen, Chairman, IEEE Students Chapter MEC for details.


  XMECians to conduct a seminar on Higher Education in the USA...
There will be a seminar on Higher Studies in the USA to be held at Model Engineering College Campus on the 27th of December, 2002. The seminar will be followed by a talk on the MBA Institutes in India and Management Education in India. The intended audience for the same are the students of MEC. All faculty and XMECians are cordially invited to grace the occasion. The seminar is slated to begin at 10 am in the morning. The talk will be facilitated by XMECians. They include Joby Babu (EE 94-98), Praveen Gopalakrishnan (EE 94-98) and Renish Pynadath (EE 94-98).


  MEC to conduct Training Programme on Linux/GNU...
Model Engineering College will conduct a short term training programme on GNU/Linux Operating System and its Effective use in Computing Curricula. The course is aimed primarily at the Teaching faculty of Engineering Colleges. The course is sponsored by Indian Society for Technical Education and All India Council for Technical Education. The training programme will be held from the 5th of August to the 8th of August 2002. Prof. Jyothi John, Principal and Head of the Department of Computer Engineering, Model Engineering College will be the course coordinator.


  XMEC US West Coast pitches in funds for the Technical Fest EXCEL2k2 ...
The USA West Coast Chapter of XMEC has decided to participate in the organizing of the two day Technical Fest EXCEL2K2 this year. The West Coast Chapter has released a fund of Rs 12000 in favour of the organising committee of the Collegiate Fest. This fund will enable the organisers to popularize the Technical Fest. XMEC West Chapter members hope that interactons between the Alumni and students such as this will help the College in its continued pursuit of technical excellence.


  MEC to organize Technical Fest EXCEL2k2 in college..
The students of MEC are organising a two day Technical Fest named EXCEL2k2 in the college beginning March 8th 2002. The fest will be organised by the IEEE chapter of MEC. The two day fest will encomprise of Design Competitions at a collegiate level and an exhibition. The students of Model Engineering college will be showcasing some of their ideas at the various stalls. The Design Competitions will include a Software Design contest, Hardware Design apart from a Technical Quiz. Students from the various college in Kerala are slated to participate in the show. All events boast of attractive cash incentives. The technical fest EXCEL 2k1 held last year was a success and the students look forward to making this meet a success too. For further details, one can contact the The Convener, EXCEL2k2, Model Engineering College, Kochi. Visit the College at www.mec.ac.in


  XMEC site inauguration at College.
The site for XMEC was inaugurated at the College on January 26th 2002. The site aims to serve the networking needs of the Alumni of Model Engineering College. The site is funded by the alumni of Model Engineering College and is hosted in partnership with Marlabs Software (P) Ltd. The site in its complete form will facilitate Discussion Boards, Chats Sessions,Polls and Access to Job opportunities. The first phase will however address the issue of Networking and creating an updated database of all the MECians around the globe..
