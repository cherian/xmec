  College Chapter.

The College chapter comprises all the XMECians who work and live in Kerala. All XMECians working at Model Engineering College, in Kochi, Thiruvanathapuram and other cities of Kerala are part of this chapter. The Principal and College faculty are members of this Chapter. This Chapter controls and accounts the current funds of XMEC

Chapter Strength : 30
