  XMECians selected as Most Valuable Professionals by Microsoft
Roshan James and Pooja Malpani have been nominated as Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVP). This is in recognition of the their commendable work in the development in Microsoft Technologies. Roshan James and Pooja Malpani work with Cognizant Technology Solutions, Bangalore. Roshan and Pooja did their B.Tech in Computer Engineering during 1998-2002. They were part of the MEC team that was adjusted the Runners Up at the National Intecollegiate Microsoft Techincal Challenge.


  XMECian is International Student Ambassador in Australian University
Mithun Koshy Alexander has be nominated the President, Postgraduate and Research Students' Association 2002-03 at the Australian National University, Canberra Australia. He has been nominated the Canberra International Student Ambassador and Student Coordinator in the year 2002. Mithun is currently doing his Masters at the Universitiy. Mithun did his BTech in Biomedical Engineering in the year 1995 - 99.


  XMECian in IIM gets the highest pay in Campus Placements
Krishnaswamy A was offered the highest salary by a company named Triniti Corporation, during campus placement that took place at the Indian Institute of Management, Kozhikode. Krishnaswamy did his BTech in Biomedical Engineering during 1994 - 1998.
