  Expense Chart

In this section, we would like to give an idea about the expenses involved in the application process and this can determine the number of schools one can apply for.

Details

  • Applying for transcripts from the University
  • Application fee for the US Schools
  • Miscellaneous Expenses (Mail, Courier)
  • GRE/ TOEFL

Expense

  • Rs. 2000
  • Rs. 2500 ($50)
  • Rs. 500
  • Rs. 10,000($200)

If one plans to apply in N schools, the total expense will be around

Rs. 10000 + 5000 x N

The best recommendation will be to apply in 4-5 schools (i.e., N = 4 or 5). Our recommendation for a selection based on school ranking is as follows.

Make sure to add at least one-maybe two schools in the range of 50-100 ranking, so that one is almost sure of admission.

Then select 1-2 schools that one is really interested in, those in the ranking of 25-50 (most state universities fall in this category).

Lastly, depending on whether one gets a very good GRE score, apply to one of those places in the top league (1-20) like MIT, Stanford, Berkeley, Caltech and so on.

If one looks at the previous years GRE/TOEFL scores, one gets an idea about the admission requirements. Please feel free to contact us at Higher Studies@XMEC especially regarding school selection and we can give one more pointers.
