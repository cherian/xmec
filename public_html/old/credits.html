Long History of Credit

Just as Rome was not built in a day, the idea for an alumni website did not take shape out of the blue. It has been an arduous journey through the long dark tunnel before it finally saw the proverbial light of day. www.xmec.net would never have been possible but for all the support of XMECians, MECians and faculty.
