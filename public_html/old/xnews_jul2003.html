  The XMEC Hyderabad Chapter meet on January 26th, 2003....
The first ever meet of the Hyderabad Chapter of XMEC will be held on January 26th, 2003. This would be the first chapter get together in the New Year. All XMECians are invited to take part in this get together. Interested XMECians are reqested to confirm their presence by mailing to Coordinator, XMEC Hyderabad Chapter


  XMECian on BBC MasterMind India Quiz....
Alphus Pathrose has reached the Semifinal round of the popular MasterMind India aired on the BBC Channel. The Semifinal episode will be aired at 10pm, Thursday December 19th, 2002 on the BBC World Channel. There will be repeat telecasts at 10am and 10pm on Sunday December 19th, 2002. Mastermind India is the indian version of the ever popular Mastermind Quiz show. The show is hosted by Sudhartha Basu.
Alphus Pathrose did his Engineering in Electronics and hails from the 5th Batch of MEC. Alphus pursues a career in business in Kerala.


  The XMEC Chennai Chapter meet on December 8th, 2002...
XMECians of the Chennai Chapter got together on December 8th 2002 at the MGM Quality Inn Beach resort, East Coast Road, Muttukad, Chennai. The Chennai chapter has grown to a strength of 66, proudly claiming to be the first XMEC Chapter in a metropolitan city in India. Apart from nomination the office bearers for the next financial year and other serious discussion, the get together was an occassion for fun and frolic. Details of the meeting can be found in the Chapter Reports. Minutes of the meeting are available on the Discussion Boards


  The USA East Coast Chapter meet on September 21st, 2002...
XMECians of the East Coast Chapter met on September 21st 2002 at the sprawling Johnson's Park in New Jersey. Thirty one members with kith and kin attended this year's meet. Apart from the celebrations, the XMECians discussed on ways and means to help new alumni coming to that part of the world. One can contact them at xmec_east@yahoogroups.com. XMECians can find a more detailed report in the Chapter Activities


  The 10th Batch have become XMECians...
Students of the 1998 - 2002 Batch have formally joined XMEC during the later half of September 2002. Many for them have been placed in companies like Cognizant Technology Solutions, Larsen & Turbro, Paxonet Communications, Infosys Technologies Ltd, Siemens Information Systems, Poornam etc.


  Vinitha Suresh bags prestigious Innovator Asia Award.
Vinitha Suresh was adjudged Innovator Asia by the prestigious EDN Asia Magazine. Vinitha works for Paxonet Systems in the field of IC design. Vinitha is part of the first breed of engineers who passed out of Model Engineering College. She did her Electronics Engineering during the period 1989-93. You can refer to the EDN news item for details.


  Product designed by an XMECian bags award.
Shahnavas M S (EE 93-97) has bagged the award for Best Design in the Steel Furniture Design Competition. Shahnavas's Design represented from Featherlite Products Ltd., has been installed in MG Road ( Between Queens Road and Kumble Circle ) for public reactions. Shahnavas did his Masters in Design from the Indian Institute of Science. He works for GE Power Controls.


  Onam Celebrations of Bangalore Chapter.
The Bangalore Chapter of XMEC, which is the single largest group in XMEC, celebrated Onam in grand tradition on October 7th 2001. The get-together took place at Eastern Cultural Association (ECA) Auditorium, Indiranagar, Bangalore. The get together brought together over hundred XMECians with XMECians from First Batch to the Ninth Batch participating.


  The 9th batch joins the active ranks of XMEC.
The Batch of 1997-2001 has joined the active ranks of XMEC. They are concentrated mainly in Chennai and Trivandrum followed by Bangalore in various firms like Cognizant Technological Solutions, iNautix, US Software, IBS Solutions, CISCO, IBM, Robert Bosch India,Wipro and SIEMENS.


  Informal get together of XMEC US East Coast Chapter held.
The US East Coast Chapter of XMEC held its first informal get together on September 8th 2001. An executive committee is yet to be nominated. Joby Babu will be the co-ordinator during this interim period.


  XMEC celebrates Onam in the Silicon Valley.
The alumni chapters in United States of America represent the largest group outside India. The US West Coast Chapter of XMEC held its first meeting which also marked the Chapter's Onam get together. An executive committee under the leadership of Faisal P A was nominated to conduct its affairs on the West Coast.


  The first meeting of Chennai Chapter of XMEC met on May 1st 2001.
The Chennai Chapter of XMEC held its first meeting in accordance with the decision taken at the college session on May 1st 2001. An executive committee under the leadership of Ajith Narayanan was nominated to conduct its affairs in Chennai. The strength of the Chennai chapter has increased significantly as most from the 1997-2001 Batch are placed in Chennai.


  XMECians in Bangalore formally meet on June 3rd 2001.
The first meeting of XMECians Bangalore with aim of forming the Bangalore Chapter was held on June 3rd 2001. Manu Sreenivasan was nominated as the President of the Bangalore Chapter. A working committee was also nominated to drive the activities of the Alumni. A Ganamela was also held by XMECians on this occasion.


  Extraordinary Session of the Alumni Association held at college.
Model Engineering College played host to an extraordinary session of the Alumni Association on May 1st 2001. This meeting which gave a new lease of life to the hitherto dormant association, has mooted a revamp of its goals and activities as well as the plan of action for the coming financial year.


  Batch of 1993-1997 Electronics held a seminar at the College.
The Batch of 1993-1997 Electronics held a seminar at the College to create an awareness among the MEC students of the current trends in the IT World. The whole exercise aimed at boosting the morale and guiding the final year students as they prepare for Campus Placements. The team was led by SibiRaj B.
