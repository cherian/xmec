  Freshers.

It is often the toil of the past that begets the rewards of the future, and we at XMEC certainly identify with all fresh engineers who are on the look out for a break. Many of the MEC alumni themselves have been through the initial lean patches, having had to slog it out through many an anxious day, before their efforts bore fruit. Hence it is no surprise that XMECians have often played vital roles in helping freshers in their search for a career, through information regarding new openings, guidance, motivation and support. XMEC hopes to continue this effort in an organized fashion. Freshers can interact with XMEC by sending a mail to Freshers@XMEC

  Higher Studies.

A plethora of opportunities and options await an engineer fresh out of college, all of which will help to hone his skills and prepare himself for the days ahead. He may choose to enroll in an array of post graduate courses available both within our country and abroad, in the technical stream (preliminary exams are GATE or GRE) take up a course in management (CAT, GMAT, other institute-specific entrance exams) or pursue industry specific design courses (M-Des @ IISc, Master of Design @ NID) etc. Check out the article by XMECians on Graduation in the USA. XMECians can help you with suggestions and advice regarding the best options. Prospective applicants can voice their queries and concerns to Higher Studies@XMEC

  Looking for a change..?

XMECians are proud to be associated with the cream of the global list of companies in the Information Technology and Medical Instrumentation Domain. A casual reader may be pardoned for considering the list of employer companies of XMEC as a Fortune 100 listing! XMECians also work in many of the premier Institutions of the country associated with cutting edge research and development work in Software, Biomedical Instrumentation and Electronics. XMECians often share valuable information regarding requirements for experienced hands in their respective organisations, and are always willing to assist any of their fellow alumni looking for a change. You can submit your resume below. If your credentials match with the requirements of the respective company, you will be intimated at the earliest. XMEC does not however assure success each time you submit your resume. Post your details at Change @XMEC
