June 10th MEET - Chennai Chapter - REPORT

The inauguration of the Chennai Chapter of XMEC was held at Moubrows Inn Chennai. The enthusiastic response that came from MEC-ians had already buoyed up the organizers' spirits. Twenty XMEC-ians from the total group of 25 odd XMECians in Chennai attended the meet.

The meeting commenced with the formal registration of all the members. It was but natural that the next one hour was spend in renewing old relations as it had been some time since most of them had met each other.

There was a silent prayer on commencement of the official meeting. The welcome-speech was delivered by Prinu S. Nazeem. Representatives of the Bangalore chapter Shyam Bhaskara Menon and Gopi Krishnan P briefed the gathering on the discussions and decisions taken at the XMEC meeting in Banglore and the extra-ordinary meeting in Kochi.

Sunil A moderated an open forum to discuss all relevant issues, and then to decide the hierarchy of the XMEC-Chennai Chapter. The discussion that followed and the relevant points that came up have been encapsulated as minutes. This was followed by a nomination session where-in the members made a flexible excutive-committee comprising of 4 members.

The executive committee members then met to decide the future agenda for the Chennai Chapter. The rest of the group had a small game of Tambola - the ultimate party game. Nithya was the lucky lady on whom Mother Luck smiled . The executive committee then briefed us on the major decisions made.

Minutes of the XMEC Chennai Chapter meeting June 10, 2001.

1. How to tap the knowledge of Alumni members to benefit the students of college.
a. Through XMEC web site. Alumni members should have given a chance to register themselves as Gurus of some technology or business domain.
b. The students of the college can take help from these Xmecians for their project needs.

2. How to raise the funds for the activities of chapters as well as for the functionality of the XMEC.
a. Registration fees / Annual fees (Can be uniform across the Chapters in India.)
b. How this fund can be utilized for the benefit of college and Xmecians.

3. Quality of engineers passing out from the college. a. The teaching staff has to be strict in Lab as well as in the lecture hall.
b. A good relation ship b/w staff and students. Some kind of awareness among staff and students is necessary.
c. Enhancing the facilities of the college. (Library, Lab and Software tools....)
d. More talks and interactions by Xmecians with the students of the college regarding the Industry trends and Technology trends.

4. The brochures / Palm lets used by the college for placement activities.
a. These palm lets can be printed in a yearly basis.
b. Copies of theses palm lets/brochure can be distributed to various organizations through the chapters/Chapter Members.

5. Chapter meetings.
a. Decided to meet in a half yearly fashion. The meeting in the month of March and a meeting during the Onam.
b. The Executive committee is decided to meet in every two months.

6. Target or aim for the XMEC activities.
a. A target/aim should be there for the Activities of XMEC.
b. Yearly plan / a sort of budget should be there as a whole.
c. This plan will describe the targets for the current financial year.

7. A web site for the College can be funded by Alumni and can be developed by the students of the college.
