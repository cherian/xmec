  Who can Register ?

Many of the facilities of this site are exclusive to the alumni of MEC, and hence will not be available to the general internet user. Any inconvenience caused is regretted. All XMECians who have earned their degrees from Model Engineering College are members by default. They need not follow any separate registration procedure.

  Did not receive Login ?

All XMECians who are members of the XMEC Yahoogroups should have received their Login and temporary password through a personal mail. If you haven't received your mail, please notify the Moderator

  Forgot Password ?

In the event of a forgotten password or Login, please send a mail to the Webmasters

  Other Issues?

In case you encounter any other problems, send a mail to the Webmasters. Kindly mention the problem encountered clearly and it will be rectified as early as possible.
